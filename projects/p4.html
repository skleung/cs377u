Project 4: Final

P4 is your chance to go deep, iterate, and create your prototype. It culminates with the final presentations during dead week.

Goal

P4 pulls it all together: visual communication, interaction design, and prototype implementation. Continue with your P3 teams to aim for a realized prototype that has evolved based on user feedback.

Schedule and Feedback

Below is our recommended timeline for Project 4. Remember that it may take several iterations of feedback on submissions before you and your team are ready to declare a milestone complete. So aim to submit your first version of each milestone at least one studio day before the deadline you want to hit.

  • Feb. 29 (week 9): P4.I — functional prototype
  • Mar. 4 (week 9): P4.II — user feedback
  • Mar. 10 (week 10) @ 10am, hard deadline: P4.III — final prototype and presentations
  • Mar. 14 (finals) @ 11:59pm, hard deadline: P4.IV - team feedback and final report

Grading rubric

Each of the three milestones is worth five points. You will receive five points for completing the assignment satisfactorily, submitting it, and your whole team bringing printouts to class for feedback.

We will grade both the final presentation and the final submission summatively.
